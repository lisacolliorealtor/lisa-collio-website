What Industries and Employers Are in Elkhart, Indiana?

Elkhart's economy is defined by one industry more than any other: recreational vehicle (RV) manufacturing.

Elkhart County as a whole is often called the RV Capital of the World, and Elkhart itself is home to some of the largest names in the industry. Thor Industries — one of the largest RV manufacturers in the world, and owner of brands including Airstream, Jayco, and Heartland — is headquartered in Elkhart. Forest River, another major manufacturer, is also based in the city. Lippert Components, a significant supplier to the RV industry, operates extensively in the Elkhart and nearby Middlebury area.

Together, this concentration of manufacturers and suppliers means the large majority of RVs sold in the United States trace back to this part of Indiana — and a substantial share of local employment connects to this industry, whether directly on a manufacturing floor or through the broader supply chain.

For anyone considering a move to Elkhart for RV-industry work, or simply wanting to understand what drives the local economy, this manufacturing base is central to daily life in the city.
