What Industries and Employers Are in the Goshen Area?

Understanding the local economy is a useful part of researching any place you might move to — and in Goshen's case, the economic story is closely tied to the broader Elkhart County region.

Elkhart County is home to the majority of U.S. recreational vehicle (RV) manufacturing — commonly cited as more than 80% of global RV production concentrated in the Elkhart–Middlebury–Goshen area. Major manufacturers with operations in the region include Thor Industries (headquartered in Elkhart and owner of brands like Airstream and Jayco), Forest River, Keystone RV, Lippert Components, and others. This concentration of manufacturing means a significant share of local jobs — directly or through supporting industries — connect back to RV production.

Within Goshen itself, two employers stand out locally: Goshen College, the small liberal arts college that anchors much of the city's cultural and educational life, and IU Health Goshen Hospital, the area's primary hospital and a major local employer.

Downtown Goshen also supports a healthy base of small, independently owned businesses — part of what gives the compact downtown district its character.

For anyone relocating for a job in the RV industry specifically, Goshen's location within this manufacturing region is one of the most practical facts to know about the area.
