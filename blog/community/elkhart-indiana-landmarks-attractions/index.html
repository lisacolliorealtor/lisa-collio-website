What Landmarks and Attractions Does Elkhart, Indiana Have?

Elkhart offers a mix of cultural and recreational landmarks that reflect both its history and its identity as a manufacturing hub.

The Lerner Theatre is one of the city's most recognized landmarks — a historic performance venue in downtown Elkhart that continues to host concerts and events. Wellfield Botanic Gardens offers a different kind of destination, with cultivated gardens and green space for residents and visitors.

Reflecting the city's manufacturing identity directly, the RV/MH Hall of Fame & Museum sits on a roughly 40-acre campus in Elkhart and draws more than 15,000 visitors annually. Beyond its museum exhibits, the campus also hosts industry events tied to the region's RV manufacturing business.

Together, these landmarks give Elkhart a combination of historic arts and culture, green space, and a direct connection to the industry that shapes the region.
