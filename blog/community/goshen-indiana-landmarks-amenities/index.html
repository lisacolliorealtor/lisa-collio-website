What Landmarks and Amenities Does Goshen Have?

Goshen offers a mix of local landmarks and easy access to attractions throughout Elkhart County.

Within the city, the Old Bag Factory is one of the most recognized landmarks — a converted historic industrial building now home to shops, studios, and small businesses. Downtown Goshen itself functions as a kind of ongoing amenity, with its closely clustered layout and regular events like First Fridays bringing the community together on a recurring basis.

Beyond the city limits, Goshen residents have convenient access to attractions throughout the wider county, including Wellfield Botanic Gardens and the historic Lerner Theatre, both in nearby Elkhart, and the RV/MH Hall of Fame & Museum, which draws more than 15,000 visitors annually and also hosts industry events on its roughly 40-acre campus.

Together, these local and regional landmarks give Goshen residents a combination of small-town amenities close to home and easy access to larger attractions nearby.
