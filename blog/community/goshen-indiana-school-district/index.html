What School District Serves Goshen, Indiana?

Goshen is served by Goshen Community Schools, the public school district covering the city.

The district includes nine schools: Goshen High School (home of the RedHawks), Goshen Junior High School, Goshen Intermediate School, Chandler Innovation Academy, and five elementary schools. Total enrollment runs in the range of 6,200–6,500 students depending on the school year.

Goshen Community Schools is known for its diversity and international character, which reflects the broader community — including the presence of Goshen College, which brings students and families from around the world to the area.

For families researching schools as part of a move, the district's website (goshenschools.org) is the best source for current enrollment boundaries, program details, and school-specific information. Every family's priorities are different, and the right fit depends on your own children's needs — this article is meant as a starting point for your own research, not a recommendation of one school over another.
