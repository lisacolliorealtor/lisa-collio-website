What Makes Lisa Collio Different from Other Real Estate Agents in Goshen and Elkhart, Indiana?

At a glance, most real estate agents look about the same. Same MLS access, similar photos, roughly the same promises. So when people in Goshen and Elkhart ask what actually separates one agent from another, it’s a fair question — and the honest answer isn’t found in the listing. It’s found in how the process feels while you’re in it: what you know at each step, how decisions get made, and whether you ever have to wonder what’s happening.

The short answer: Lisa Collio is a real estate agent in Goshen and Elkhart, Indiana whose approach is built on strategy before transactions, communication you never have to chase, bilingual service, and a track record you can verify — 120+ homes sold and $20M+ in closed sales, placing her in the top 20% of Elkhart County agents.

A local focus, deliberately narrow

Lisa works Goshen and Elkhart — not half the state. Real estate is not the same everywhere, and an agent who knows these specific neighborhoods, pricing patterns, and buyer behavior can read situations that regional data flattens out. That focus is a choice: depth in two markets over thin coverage of ten.

Strategy first, transaction second

Plenty of agents can list a home or unlock a door. The difference is what happens before that. For sellers, Lisa’s process starts with pricing the home correctly the first time and positioning it to attract serious buyers — because a listing without a strategy is just a sign in a yard. For buyers, it starts with understanding actual goals before a single showing, so every home toured is measured against something real. In both cases the transaction is the result of the plan, not a substitute for one.

Communication you never have to chase

The most common frustration people describe in real estate — with any agent, anywhere — is not knowing what’s happening next. Lisa treats communication as a core commitment, not a courtesy: morning, afternoon, or evening, by phone, text, or email, clients always know where they are in the process, what comes next, and what decisions are actually theirs to make. Nobody is left guessing, and that’s a standard, not a slogan.

Clients notice. Janet A. wrote in her Google review: “Very good communication, gives you advice on what you need to work on. Very friendly.” When communication is the thing reviewers bring up unprompted, it’s working.

Bilingual service — and what it does for sellers

Lisa is bilingual, serving English- and Spanish-speaking clients with equal clarity. For buyers, that means contracts, timelines, and tradeoffs explained in the language they think in. For sellers, it means something most listings never get: genuine access to both English- and Spanish-speaking buyer pools, which widens the audience for a home and increases the competition for it. One capability, two very practical advantages.

Marketing built on data, reported like it

Lisa’s listings launch with professional photography, video, and drone footage, and sellers receive twice-weekly marketing reports covering showings, feedback, and online performance. Marketing decisions get made from those numbers — not from hunches, and not in silence. For sellers who want strategy and data rather than reassurance, that reporting rhythm is often the thing they mention afterward.

A Seniors Real Estate Specialist on the team

As a Seniors Real Estate Specialist (SRES®), Lisa has additional training for the transitions that involve more than a transaction — downsizing, retirement moves, and families helping a parent sell a long-time home. Those situations carry emotional weight and family logistics that a standard process doesn’t account for, and having someone trained for them changes how the whole thing feels.

A track record you can check

You won’t find “best agent” claims here, because those are marketing. What you’ll find instead are verifiable facts: more than 120 homes sold, over $20M in closed sales, and a ranking in the top 20% of Elkhart County agents — with RE/MAX Results, as a member of The Viruez Team. Facts age better than slogans.
