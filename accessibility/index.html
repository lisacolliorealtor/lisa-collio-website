Accessibility

Everyone should be able to use this website. lisacolliorealtor.com is built and maintained with the goal of meeting the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and the Site is reviewed and updated on an ongoing basis to keep improving — including text alternatives for images, sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation, and pages that work well with screen readers.

If any part of the Site is difficult for you to use, or if you'd like help searching for real estate in another format, please reach out — by phone at (574) 370-5410 or by email at lisacolliorealtor@gmail.com. Feedback about accessibility barriers is genuinely welcome and gets acted on.
