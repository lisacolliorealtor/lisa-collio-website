Fair Housing Statement

Lisa Collio, Real Estate Agent, and this website fully support the principles of the federal Fair Housing Act (Title VIII of the Civil Rights Act of 1968, as amended), the Indiana Fair Housing Act, and the REALTOR® Code of Ethics. Housing discrimination has no place in this business — in any listing, any showing, any negotiation, or any word published on this Site.

That means equal professional service for everyone, without regard to race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, or ancestry. It also shapes how this Site is written: property descriptions and community pages describe homes, neighborhoods, and amenities — never the kind of person who “belongs” in them.

As a member of the National Association of REALTORS®, Lisa is bound by a Code of Ethics that in several respects goes beyond what the law requires, and this Site is built to that stricter standard.

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination, you can file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development at hud.gov/fairhousing or by calling 1-800-669-9777, or with the Indiana Civil Rights Commission at in.gov/icrc.
