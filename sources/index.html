About this page

This page lists the sources used to research and write the community and area content on this website, organized by the page or article each source supports.

This list is not yet complete for the whole website. It currently covers the Goshen community and relocation pages listed below. As new pages and articles are published, their sources will be added here. If a page on this site isn't yet listed, its sources have not yet been added to this page — that does not mean the content is unsourced, only that this page hasn't caught up to it yet.

This information is deemed reliable but not guaranteed. Sources listed here were accurate and current as of the date shown for each page. Community details — business names, hours, events, and similar facts — change over time, and a source being listed here is not a guarantee that the fact drawn from it remains current today. Named businesses, events, and similar details are reviewed at least annually; if you notice something out of date, please let us know.

This page does not cover market or property data. Market statistics are sourced separately, directly from the Elkhart County Board of REALTORS® (ECBOR), with the source and date shown for each figure on the Market Stats page. This page also does not list the internal compliance and brand documents that govern how this site is written (fair housing rules, brand standards, and similar) — only the sources of the facts the site reports.
