Buying a Home in Elkhart, Indiana

Elkhart’s housing market covers a lot of ground — established neighborhoods near downtown, newer developments on the edges of town, and everything in between. That range is exactly what makes it worth understanding before you start touring homes: the right property for one buyer’s budget and goals can be completely wrong for another’s, even at the same price point.

Lisa Collio works with Elkhart buyers to make sense of that range — pointing out where the opportunities are, what a given price actually gets you in a given neighborhood, and how to move through the process without second-guessing every step.
