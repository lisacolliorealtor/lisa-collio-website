What the process actually looks like

These are the practical steps that unfold inside the Execute step of The Next Chapter Method™ — the on-the-ground work of finding and buying your home.

01. Get Pre-Approved First

Do this before anything else. Seriously. A lot of buyers skip it and end up losing a home they wanted because someone else was already ready to go and they weren't.

02. Figure out what you're looking for

This sounds easier than it is. Most people have a list in their head, but when you start looking at real homes in a real market, that list shifts pretty quickly. Lisa helps you sort through what genuinely matters to you versus what just sounds good, so you're not wasting weekends on houses that were never going to work anyway.

03. Go see them in person

Visiting properties allows buyers to evaluate layout, condition, and location.

04. When you find the right one, move

The homes worth buying don't sit around waiting. You have to move fast.

05. Inspection and appraisal

This is the part where you find out what you're really getting into. The inspection looks at what's actually going on with the property. The appraisal makes sure the number you agreed to holds up.

06. Closing day

You sign everything, the deal becomes official, and someone hands you the keys. If the process has been handled well from the beginning, this day usually feels more like a relief than a stressor — which is exactly how it should feel.
